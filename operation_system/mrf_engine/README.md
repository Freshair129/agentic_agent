## 📋 Overview

**MRF (Metacognitive Re-contextualization Framework)** is EVA's "Strategic Reframing" engine. It implements the **Umbrella Principle**: instead of trying to "solve" uncontrollable or paradoxical events, the MRF shifts the context to one where the event can be accepted and transcended.

---

## ⚙️ 7-Layer Processing Pipeline

1. **Layer 1: Literal**: Basic surface meaning and entities.
2. **Layer 2: Emotional**: Underlying emotional undertone.
3. **Layer 3: Symbolic (APM)**: Metaphorical and archetypal transformation.
4. **Layer 4: Relational**: Analysis of power dynamics and interaction patterns.
5. **Layer 5: Narrative**: Positioning the event within a larger life or session arc.
6. **Layer 6: Meta**: Self-awareness of why this specific interpretation was chosen (Paradox Detection).
7. **Layer 7: Transcendental**: Extraction of wisdom and deployment of the **Umbrella** for safety.

---

## 📂 Structure

- **`mrf_engine.py`**: The 7-layer interpretation pipeline logic.
- **`MRF_Concept.md`**: Philosophical framework and technical specification.
- **`configs/`**: Enabled/disabled flags for each interpretation layer.

## 📐 Governance

- **System Authority**: Capable of triggering system-wide safety deployments (Umbrella).
- **Paradox Resolution**: The primary tool for managing "cognitive dissonance" or conflicting user inputs.
