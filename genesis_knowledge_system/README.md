**Directory**: `genesis_knowledge_system/`  
**Purpose**: Authority for static wisdom, structural logic, and archetypal grounding.

---

## 📋 Overview

The **Genesis Knowledge System (GKS)** is EVA's "Collective Unconscious" — a curated repository of foundational knowledge, algorithms, and philosophies that define her reasoning framework. Unlike the dynamic episodic memory, GKS is the "Source of Wisdom."

---

## 📂 The Genesis Blocks

GKS is organized into specialized JSON "Blocks":

1. [**Master Block**](Master_Block.json): The top-level index of all knowledge objects.
2. [**Algorithm (How)**](Algorithm_How_Genesis_Block.json): Procedural knowledge and reasoning logic.
3. [**Concept (Why)**](Concept_Why_Genesis_Block.json): Philosophical foundations and definitions.
4. [**Framework (Where)**](Framework_Genesis_Block.json): Structural and architectural knowledge.
5. [**Protocol (Process)**](Protocol_Process_Genesis_Block.json): Interaction and operational rules.
6. [**Parameter (What)**](Parameter_What_Genesis_Block.json): Quantitative constants and variables.
7. [**Safety Block**](Safety_Block.json): Gating and alignment principles.

---

## 🛠️ Core Components

- **`gks_loader.py`**: Handles loading and merging of JSON blocks.
- **`gks_standardizer.py`**: Enforces the **4D Rule** (Algo/Concept/Frame/Proto) and validates schemas.
- **`nexus_mind/`**: The logic center for connecting retrieved knowledge to active reasoning.

---

## 📐 Governance

- **Immutable Foundation**: Genesis blocks are typically read-only during runtime.
- **Belief Revision**: Sphere-level wisdom from MSP can be proposed for GKS integration via the **8-8-8 Protocol**.
- **Grounding Law**: No response should contradict the principles defined in the `Safety_Block`.
